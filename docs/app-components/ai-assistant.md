# AI Assistant

Brainy comes with a built-in AI Assistant designed to supercharge your study
workflow — helping you create materials faster and get more out of your
documents without ever leaving the app.

The AI Assistant is available via the popup in the **bottom right corner** of
the screen. Click it to open a chat interface where you can ask questions,
request study materials, or interact with your uploaded content in natural language.

## What the AI Can Do

**Generate Flashcards**
Simply describe a topic and ask the AI to create flashcards for you.
For example: *"Create 10 flashcards on the French Revolution"* — and Brainy will
generate a ready-to-study card set that you can add directly to any file.
The AI is optimized to follow the learning rules described in 
[this link](https://super-memory.com/articles/20rules.htm).

**Answer Questions from Your Documents**
Upload a file and the AI will read and analyze it for you. Ask it to summarize
key concepts, explain difficult sections, or turn the content into a structured
set of flashcards — all from within the chat popup.

**Context-Aware Assistance**
When you are in the editor or reviewer, the AI can see which card you are currently
viewing and use it as context for your questions or requests. This makes it easy to
ask follow-up questions about a specific card or generate related flashcards on the fly.

**Aware of Your Files and Folders**
The AI has visibility into your files and folders, so you can ask it to create
flashcards directly inside a specific file. For example: *"Add 5 flashcards on
photosynthesis to my Biology notes file"* — and Brainy will place them exactly where
you want without any manual copying.

## Uploading Documents

You can attach documents directly in the AI chat popup. Once uploaded, the AI
can reference the document's content in its responses, making it easy to build 
study materials from lecture notes, textbooks, research papers, and more.

## Supported AI Providers

Brainy supports two AI providers:

- **Ollama** — run powerful language models locally on your own machine. Your data stays private and no internet connection is required.
- **OpenAI** — connect using your OpenAI API key to use models like GPT-5.2-mini and other OpenAI-hosted models.

## Turning Off the AI Assistant

If you prefer not to use the AI features, the assistant can be disabled entirely
in **Settings**. Once turned off, the chat popup will no longer appear and no
AI-related processing will take place.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Work faster in the editor with these shortcuts:

| Shortcut | Action |
|---|---|
| `Ctrl + J` / `Cmd + J` | Toggle AI chat |
